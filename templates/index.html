Details

Mercury is a text chat app for project collaborators and study groups. It is a student project based on Discord. It can bring people together through a love of productivity and efficiency. Once there, they can join a chat they've been invited to or they can create private groups and invite their friends to collaborate or study together. This app is special because each group can be further divided into sub-groups or fields, created for the sole purpose of dividing tasks and subjects, and avoiding clutter. They can be used by any member of the group. Personal messages are also available.
